Command Brief
Drag a box to select workers or guards.
Hold Shift to add squads, or hold Ctrl to remove them.
Right click a cobalt vein to start harvesting.
Right click terrain to reposition selected units.
Right click with no selection to move the rally point.
Left click the minimap to instantly recenter the camera.
Middle click anywhere to snap the camera to that world position.
Recruit workers and guards with Q / E. For structures, press R / T or click the build card, then click a highlighted pad. Right click or Esc cancels placement.
Mine 320 ore before the HQ is destroyed. F1 hides this panel and R or Enter restarts.
